About Mangalayatan University Online Programme

Mangalayatan University is an innovative and dynamic institution of higher education located in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India. The university provides a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Management, Humanities, Sciences, Law, and Education.

Mangalayatan University started its online program keeping in mind the need for quality education and reaching out to students in every nook and corner of the country. All the courses are well-researched and designed by the campus academicians and vetted by industry experts. It is specially designed to cater to the needs of the students and corporate community.
